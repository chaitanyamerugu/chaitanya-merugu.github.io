05 — Responsible AI Framework

SAP HCM Ethics Integration

Principle 01

Data Minimisation

HR bias detection models operate on anonymised infotype structures — protected attributes are analysed for disparity impact only, never used as predictive features in production automation.

Principle 02

Explainability by Design

All bias detection outputs are designed to be interpretable by HR professionals without ML expertise — using plain-language disparity reports rather than black-box scores.

Principle 03

Human-in-the-Loop

The framework positions AI as a detection and flagging layer — final HR decisions remain with human reviewers. Automated systems surface risk; people resolve it.

Principle 04

Continuous Monitoring

Bias is not a one-time audit concern. The framework specifies recurring model evaluation cadences aligned with payroll cycles and performance review periods.
